Chat commands

Quick calls from Twitch

  • !quest — 10s cooldown. Earn XP/coins, chance to drop gear, auto-equip upgrades.
  • !daily — Bigger payout every 24h. Shows remaining time if claimed.
  • !boss — Spawns or fights the shared site hazard. Reward scales with the crew.
  • !stats — Shows level, XP to next, coins, and equipped gear.
  • !top — Top 5 workers in channel (level → total XP).
  • !inv — First 5 items in your toolbelt plus equipped weapon/trinket.
  • !gamble [amt] — 50/50 smoko bet. Default 10 coins, max 200.
  • !shop — Get the Tool Depot link. Buy/sell on web to keep chat clean.
Run smooth

Pro tips

  • Keep chat spam clean: responses stay one-liners.
  • Gear drop rates: ~70% common, 23% rare, 6% epic, 1% legendary.
  • Boss HP/rewards scale to the strongest worker on shift.
  • players.json lives on disk; you won't lose progress on deploy.
